Feedband Inc.
This start up is working on a health fitness product that non-intrusively picks up your biometric data through the ears and via bluetooth transfers that data to your mobile device. An app on the mobile device then processes the live data and produces meaningful results for the user that the user can use to improve their workout performance. Users get scores if they perform their best during workouts and that score can be posted on Facebook so others can compete with the user in workouts. The product can also be used for medical monitoring by doctors.
BroBase
This is an online directory of students from different Universities. Each student is listed with their skills, interests and work experience. Other students can go to the website and search for students for help, etc. To get in contact with students, an email request can be sent through the BroBase servers.
